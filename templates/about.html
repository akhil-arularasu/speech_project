About Me


My name is Akhil Arularasu and I am a Junior at Bridgewater-Raritan High School. I have always been an avid environmental enthusiast and care about creating an eco-friendly Earth around me.

I first started composting four years ago when I realized just how much food was wasted in my own home and my community around me. Over time, I realized the benefits of doing so as I saw by my own eyes how food that would have otherwise been sent to rot at a landfill transformed (almost magically) into nutrient-rich fertilizer for my garden. I have since founded stopfoodwaste.net where I have been spreading awareness of promoting an environmentally sustainable earth through reduction of food waste through several initiatives such as a pledge drive, analysis using data science tools, infographics, and videos that are run through this site.
